ABOUT ME

The Basics

When I was just a babe, I hardly interacted with the world. Then one fateful day, my wonderful mom bought a computer for me to tinker with, and legend has it that’s when I came out of my shell and grew into myself. That’s where my fascination with tech began.

However, it’s my problem solving nature, eagerness to learn, and the need for a creative outlet that has led me to this point as a web developer.

Today, I'm a developer and designer in my favorite part of the country: San Diego! I keep myself pretty busy making web applications for myself, friends, or clients.

It hasn't always been this way. I've also been a gymnast, radio DJ, English teacher in Spain, convention coordinator, and fish factory worker. My experiences have taught me so much about myself and have only spurred my excitement of the new and unknown more. I bring this positive energy and passion to every project I take on.
