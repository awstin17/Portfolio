ABOUT ME

The Basics

Little baby me hardly interacted with the world. Then my wonderful mom bought a computer for me to tinker with, and legend has it that’s when I came out of my shell and grew into myself. That’s where the fascination with tech began.

But it’s my fervor to problem solve, eagerness to learn, and the need for a creative outlet that has led me on the path of a web developer.

I'm a developer and designer in my favorite part of the country: San Diego. Between being a student at a local coding bootcamp, helping teach at said bootcamp, and creating websites and web applications for myself and others, I stay pretty busy

In previous lives, I've been a radio DJ, English teacher in Spain, a convention coordinator, and fish factory worker. I have and will always wear multiple hats, and I bring that excitement of the new and unknown to every project I take on
